BIOGRAPHY

K.M.MANOJ KUMAR

Born: 15 March 2000, Coimbatore

Another Name: Gokul Krishnan.

Occupation: Composer, Singer and Lyricst.

K. M. Manoj Kumar was born on 15 March 2000, Manoj belong to a family from Vedapatti in the Coimbatore. His father became a goldsmith while his mother has a homemaker. Manoj's first exposure to music came when his inspired A.R.Rahman's Musical Albums. His music journey started in the class bench.
Manoj uploaded vocal songs to the internet and got positive responses from people expressing interest in his music, who also encouraged him to start singing in Tamil. Later Manoj Started a YouTube channel "K.M.Manoj" to share his works WorldWide.
